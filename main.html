TOVE JANSSON

The author of the MOOMIN books

Tove Jansson (1914-2001) was a Finnish-Swedish writer and artist, who achieved worldwide fame as the creator of the stories about the Moomins

The Moomin family and their friends live an adventurous life in the idyllic and peaceful Moominvalley. They are a family of white and roundish trolls.
